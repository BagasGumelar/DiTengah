DiTengah

Find the fairest meeting point between two locations.

How to use

  1. Decide location A and location B.
  2. Open Google Maps and search for location A or B.
  3. Right-click on the pinned location.
  4. Click the top option to copy the coordinates (latitude, longitude).
  5. Paste the coordinates for both location A and B below.
  6. Choose what kind of place you want to meet at (e.g. cafe, restaurant, mosque, brand name).
