DiTengah

Find the fairest meeting point between two locations

How to Use

  1. Decide two locations (Location A and Location B).
  2. Search each location in Google Maps.
  3. Right-click the pin and copy the coordinates.
  4. Paste both coordinates below.
  5. Enter the type or brand of place you want to meet at.
