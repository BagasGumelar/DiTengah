DiTengah

Find the fairest meeting point between two locations.

How it works

  1. Pick two locations (for example: your place and your friend’s place).
  2. Open Google Maps and search for each location.
  3. Right-click the pinned location and copy the coordinates.
  4. Paste both coordinates below.
  5. Type what you want to meet at, for example: Restaurant, coffee shop, school, hospital, mosque, or even specific location like McDonald’s.
